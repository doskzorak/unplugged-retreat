Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged Retreat?

Air travel to the nearest airport is included in the price of all of our team-building packages. Just let your facilitator know which major airport is closest to you and you will be given a price that includes this cost within 24 hours.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

Any team kind of team is welcome at Unplugged Retreats. Whether your team just needs to refocus, or find its focus, we have the program to help you get your company to the next level.

What size teams can you accommodate?

The most successful teams we have seen have been between 8 and 10 individuals. While larger groups can be successfully helped by breaking the whole into smaller parts, finding a way to make all of the parts work together may not work as intended. We welcome groups of all sizes, regardless!

Is there really no wifi available?

There is no wifi available, it is true. There are, however, dedicated rooms with a computer from which you can conduct an emergency situation, should the need arise. The point of these retreats, though, is to disconnect and grow both individually and as a team.
